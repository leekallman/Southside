Our traditional homemade recipe for RågSwag Market starts with a big old heaping loppis/second hand marknad, stirring in lots of local artfulness, add a generous helping of homecookin’ & enhance the wondrous mix with a tasty, groovin’ musical soundscape. And that’s pretty much how we’ get cookin’ in Rågsved.

RågSwag Market ❤️ Springbeast!

For RågSwag Markets premiere we will be partnering with our friends at the enormously popular Springbeast urban art/graffiti fest in nearby Snösätra. Over the last five years, Springbeast has grown to become the largest graffiti festival in Europe. The festival attracts a wide range of visitors and artists from all over and previous years has seen up to 10 000 - 15 000 visitors.

During the last weekend of May, RågSwag Market will fill Rågsved centrum with an invitingly homegrown, community driven celebration & loppis/second hand market, that will include live music &/or DJ action, a wide array of local art, tasty homecookin’ and much more!

So, please join us & lend your vital unique presence to our grand co-creation! Afterall, we couldn’t do it without y’al!! So feel welcome to set up a table & sell/recirculate your clothes, household goods, as well as exhibit & sell your art. Or just come on by & partake in the inviting smells, sounds & tastes of this vibrant, well lived in, funky, radically influential hood! 🎺🥑
